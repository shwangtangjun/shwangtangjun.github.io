About

I am a Post-doctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at The University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Zhiwen Zhang.

Prior to this, I received my B.S. degree and Ph.D. degree from the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Tsinghua University. My Ph.D. advisor was Prof. Zuoqiang Shi.

My current research focuses on semi-supervised learning, PDE-based methods on graphs and PDE-inspired neural networks.
